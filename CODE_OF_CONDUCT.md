# Code of Conduct

## Standard

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

We aim for a welcoming, harassment-free environment regardless of experience level, identity, or background.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and reviews
- Assume good intent
- Disagree with ideas, not people
- Help newcomers find their footing

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Posting others' private information
- Trolling, deliberate misdirection, or bad-faith argument

## Reporting

Email the maintainer (see git log) for any concerns. Reports are confidential.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate this code.
