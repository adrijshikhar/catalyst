### Axes

| Axis | What it measures |
|------|------------------|
| `clarity` | Can a smart non-expert understand the main point in one read? Are claims supported? Are jargon and unexplained acronyms minimal? |
| `accuracy` | Are factual claims correct? Are quantitative claims sourced? Are no straw-men or misleading framings present? |
| `brevity` | Is every paragraph load-bearing? Could the text be 30% shorter without losing meaning? |
| `hook` | Does the opening pull the reader in? Does the structure keep them moving? Is the ending payoff worth the read? |

### Score anchors (each axis 1-5)

- **5 — Exemplary.** Publish-worthy. Cite as reference.
- **4 — Solid.** Strong draft. One pass of polish away from final.
- **3 — Acceptable.** Functional. Specific structural / wording issues to address.
- **2 — Needs work.** Multiple distracting issues; reader will bounce.
- **1 — Reject.** Restart from a fresh outline.

### Critique guidance

For any axis scored ≤3, quote the specific paragraph/sentence and propose the replacement. "Tighten this section" is a critique failure; "drop sentences 3-5 and replace with: <text>" is the bar.
