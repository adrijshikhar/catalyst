Catalyst — logo variants

6 geometric marks, each on light + dark. Gradient #4F8CFF → #8B5CF6.

1 · Catalytic C
Open reaction ring spells the "C"; node + sparks at the active gap = acceleration. Most brand-literal.
monogram
2 · Reaction node
Reactants converge on an unconsumed core. Reads as "connective tissue" / hub. Symmetric, app-icon friendly.
network
3 · Alembic
Chemistry flask with a rising spark. Most literal "catalyst" metaphor; least abstract.
chemistry
4 · Accelerator
Fading chevrons + lead node = motion/acceleration. Clean, but generic (play/forward connotation).
motion
5 · Molecule spark
Benzene-style ring of bond nodes with a central spark. Chemistry + system, denser at small sizes.
molecule
6 · Catalytic cycle
Looping arrow = regenerated, not consumed (the literal definition) with a node on the path.
cycle
